SPI - Arduino UNO R3 and RF1100se pin map
--------------------

| SPI  | UNO R3 | RF1100se |
|------|--------|----------|
| VCC  | 3.3v   | 1 (vcc)  |
| GND  | GND    | 9 (gnd)  |
| MOSI | 11     | 3        |
| MISO | 12     | 5        |
| SS   | 10     | 7        | 
| SCK  | 13     | 4        |
| GDO0 |  2     | 8        |
